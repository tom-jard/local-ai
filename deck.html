Reality Check

The honest version

Local AI is real and useful, but it comes with tradeoffs.

Where it shines

  • ● Studying, drafting, brainstorming, everyday questions
  • ● Fast enough on a decent laptop
  • ● Runs on hardware you already own

Where it falls short

  • ● Not as strong as the best cloud models
  • ● Larger models can hit memory limits fast
  • ● Speed depends a lot on your hardware
  • ● The app ecosystem is still early and sometimes rough

Use local for everyday tasks and experimentation. Use cloud when you need the strongest model.

"Here's the honest version." "These are not frontier models, and that matters." "You'll run into memory limits, speed limits, and rough edges depending on your hardware." "But for a lot of everyday use and lower-stakes work, local AI is already useful." [BUILD 1] "So this isn't about replacing every cloud model." "It's about having another option for everyday tasks and experimentation." Spoken transition to next section: "And that leads to the privacy story."
